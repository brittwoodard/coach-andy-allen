Coach Andy Allen

Coach Andy Allen is a renowned coach’s coach and a teacher’s teacher with over two decades of experience in behavior, neuroscience, and performance coaching. As a skilled behaviorist and mind coach, Andy has dedicated his career to working with high-achieving individuals. He specializes in helping his clients enhance mental clarity, sharpen focus, and build resilience, using a unique blend of eastern techniques and modern neuroscience.

Andy is the Founder of the USA’s largest school of Mind Body Medicine, Yoga Veda Institute, where he has cultivated a legacy of empowering high performers through comprehensive mind-body education. Known for his strong, transformative presence, Andy’s coaching goes beyond conventional methods, addressing the deeper psychological barriers that hinder peak performance and personal fulfillment.

With a focus on elite performers, Coach Andy Allen’s mission is to help powerful individuals master their minds, overcome challenges such as stress, anxiety, and confidence issues, and unlock their full potential. Whether on the field or in the boardroom, Andy’s clients achieve lasting success and fulfillment, driven by his expertise in blending ancient wisdom with the latest advancements in neuroscience.

Coach Andy Allen is a renowned mind coach with a deep expertise in performance psychology. His unique approach fosters a state of calm, confidence, and sharp focus, empowering clients to excel in every aspect of their lives. With Andy’s guidance, you’ll develop the mental resilience and clarity needed to achieve your most ambitious goals and lead a life of sustained success and balance.
